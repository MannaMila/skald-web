What to have nearby

Your version, and one honest thought

The form asks which device and app version you used, what you tried, and one thing Skald should keep or change. Hesitation, bug, device, contact, and anonymous-quotation details are optional.

You can find the version in About & Privacy. “Not sure” is a valid answer if it is not close at hand.

Screenshots and support

The form accepts no file uploads.

If a problem needs a screenshot, send it to support@mannamila.com. Please do not include a full promotion code, the app’s Analytics ID, a child’s information, or private reading details.
